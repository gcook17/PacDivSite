Biography: Mike Patterson, N6JGA received his first ham license in 1994, and is now an Extra Class operator. He soon got involved in public service events and emergency communications. Over the years since, he has held other positions with various duties; including within his local CERT group; assisting with training courses and being a Volunteer Examiner; serving on the Pacificon Committee and on the board of directors for NARCC. He is a past president of the Mt. Diablo Amateur Radio Club and have held other positions in that club. He is also a member of other clubs within and outside the Section.
