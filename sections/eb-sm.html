Biography: Jim Siemons, W6LK, an Amateur Extra Class licensee was first licensed on April 1, 2009. He has had a strong connection with electronics and science from an early age and was the quintessential A/V geek from elementary school through college. Jim received a degree in Accounting from St. Mary's College of California, but while there and subsequent studied radio, communications, data communications, welding, and computer science.

ARRL Experience: Jim's interests in Amateur Radio are many. He has taken great interest in Elmering, EmComm, club management, contesting, digital modes, and has a very special place in his heart for PACIFICON the ARRL Pacific Division Convention. W6LK is very enthusiastic about all aspects of Amateur Radio, and believes strongly in the wisdom of KT6Y, "The only way to have a friend is to be one."

Operating Experience: Jim Siemons, W6LK, an Amateur Extra Class licensee was first licensed on April 1, 2009.

Other Activities: Jim, W6LK, is a family man that along with his very supportive XYL, Misa, KJ6BUE, (PACIFICON Chair,2016-2018), has three children: Emily, AI6OQ, Louis, and Mary.

Professional Experience: Jim was the Production Manager at KSMC radio while in college. W6LK went on to partner with his brother in a direct-mail marketing firm in Berkeley, CA. His specialty was business and database management. After retiring from his firm, Jim became involved in and ultimately purchased Elk Antennas. He now designs, produces, and markets antennas that are used all over the globe.
