Local and Section Exam Sessions

This page is a list of Volunteer Examiner Coordinators (VECs) offering test sessions in the Pacific Division. The Federal Communications Commission (FCC), which issues amateur radio licenses, works through these VECs to provide exam sessions for those wishing to get a license, or upgrade to a higher class of License.

Don't yet have your license? Then you can call the ARRL's toll-free number -- 1-800-32-NEWHAM -- for helpful information!

If you would like to have your Test Sessions listed on this page, please e-mail the PDarrl Webmaster.

San Francisco Bay Area

BAEARS holds study sessions for all license levels except Amateur Extra and exams for all levels of license.

For information go to the BAEARS web site at ► http://www.baears.com/

Folsom ARRL VE's

Folsom ARRL VEs 3rd Thurs of each month, 6 pm. No session July, Aug or Dec.

Location: TBD since Raley's closed their community room. Test sessions will resume when we find a free location near Folsom.

For information, go to ► http://ns6q.net/ns6q.net/exams/

Silicon Valley Volunteer Examiner Group (SVVEG)

The Silicon Valley VE Group offers twice-monthly amateur radio license exams in the South Bay.

We’re a friendly bunch of hams dedicated to assisting you in getting your new license or upgrade

For more information, go to ► http://www.svve.org.

Sunnyvale VEC

Test sessions throughout Northern California and Oregon, with a schedule posted on their website.

For more information, go to ► http://www.amateur-radio.org.

Kaua'i ARC

Test sessions in Kapa'a, Kaua'i, with the scheduled dates posted on their website.

For more information, go to ► http://www.kauaiarc.org.

Koolau Amateur Radio Club

Exams after every second KARC meeting in Kaneohe, Oahu. Contact Sid Sneidar, AH6HH, 261-7916 to coordinate your exam. Full Information and Oahu Testing Schedule with requirements on the website.

Las Vegas, NV

For information on ham tests in Las Vegas Valley, Pahrump Valley and other areas in Nevada, go to ► http://www.lvhamtest.us/.
